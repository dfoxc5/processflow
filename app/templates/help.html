Standardized Formatting Style
Module
Modules will be underlined (e.g. Detention)
Screens
Screens will be italicized (e.g. Population Summary)
Buttons
Buttons will be bolded (e.g. Save)
Field Titles and Data
Field titles and data will be in quotation marks (e.g. "Elm 6")

Word Icon (Use this to download a word document containing a record)
PDF Icon (Use this to open a PDF of a record)
